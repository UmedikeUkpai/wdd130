History

Yuukay Rafting company Explorers welcomes you to Lungu. East Edda's Adventure Capital is home to some of the world's finest grade 5 white water rafting and other adventure activities. Whether you are a first-timer, family or high adventure enthusiast, you’ll find your perfect white water adventure here with Yuukay River Explorers.

A river boat cruise is the perfect way to enjoy a few relaxing hours on the River Yuukay. We have a variety of cruises which all start from Yuukay Explorers River Camp at Lungu and explore the beginning of the world’s longest river
